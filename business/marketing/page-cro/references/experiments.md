# Page CRO Experiment Ideas

Comprehensive experiment catalog organized by page element. Prioritize by impact × effort before testing.

---

## Hero Section

**Headline experiments**
- Value-outcome headline vs. problem-solution headline
- Specific numbers in headline ("Save 4 hours/week") vs. generic benefit
- Short punchy headline (<8 words) vs. explanatory headline
- Question format vs. statement format
- Customer language mirror (using words from reviews) vs. marketing language

**Visual experiments**
- Product screenshot vs. lifestyle/context image
- Animated demo vs. static image
- Person looking at product vs. person looking at camera
- Dark background vs. light background
- Above-fold video autoplay vs. thumbnail-click

**CTA experiments**
- Primary button copy: "Start free trial" vs. "Get started" vs. "Try free for 14 days"
- Single CTA vs. primary + secondary CTA
- CTA button color (contrast testing)
- CTA placement: center vs. left-aligned
- Sticky CTA bar on scroll vs. static

---

## Trust Signals

**Social proof placement**
- Logo bar above fold vs. below fold
- Customer count ("Join 10,000 teams") near CTA vs. in footer
- Testimonial with photo vs. without photo
- Video testimonial vs. text testimonial
- Star rating + review count near CTA vs. dedicated reviews section

**Credibility signals**
- "As seen in" press logos vs. customer logos
- Security badges (SOC2, GDPR) placement: checkout area vs. footer
- Money-back guarantee: near CTA vs. separate section
- Team photo vs. no team photo

---

## Pricing Presentation

**Plan structure**
- 2 plans vs. 3 plans vs. 4 plans
- Most popular badge on middle tier vs. highest tier
- Feature comparison table vs. feature list per plan
- Annual/monthly toggle default: annual vs. monthly
- Price anchoring: show annual price divided by month vs. show full annual price

**Price display**
- Round number ($99) vs. just-below ($97)
- Per user/month vs. per month (total)
- Price with strikethrough discount vs. price without
- Free tier included vs. "Try free" CTA only

---

## Form Optimization

**Field count**
- Remove optional fields (test each field removal individually)
- Multi-step form (3 steps) vs. single-page form
- Email-first form vs. full form at once
- Phone number field: required vs. optional vs. removed

**Form copy**
- Submit button: "Create account" vs. "Get started free" vs. "Start my trial"
- Field labels: above field vs. placeholder text
- Add micro-copy below email field ("No credit card required")
- Add social proof near form ("Join 1,200 companies")

---

## Navigation and UX

**Navigation**
- Full navigation vs. minimal navigation (just logo) on landing pages
- Sticky navigation vs. non-sticky
- Navigation CTA button: "Sign up" vs. "Get a demo" vs. "Start free"
- Mobile: hamburger menu vs. bottom navigation bar

**Page length and structure**
- Long-form page vs. short-form page
- Section order resequencing (e.g., pricing before testimonials)
- Add/remove FAQ section
- Add/remove comparison table vs. competitor

**Exit intent**
- Exit popup: offer discount vs. offer content lead magnet vs. no popup
- Exit popup timing: immediate vs. 30-second delay
- Chat widget: proactive message trigger vs. passive icon

---

## Mobile-Specific

- Sticky CTA button (fixed to bottom of viewport)
- Single-column vs. two-column layout for feature cards
- Tap-to-call phone number vs. form-only
- Reduce text density (larger font, more whitespace)
- Simplified hero: remove video/animation, use static image

---

## Speed and Technical

- Lazy load images below fold vs. eager load all
- Remove third-party scripts from landing pages (chat, analytics)
- Next-gen image format (WebP/AVIF) vs. JPEG/PNG
- Preload hero image vs. standard loading

---

## Prioritization Matrix

| High Impact + Low Effort | High Impact + High Effort |
|--------------------------|--------------------------|
| CTA copy | Video testimonial production |
| Headline A/B test | Full page redesign |
| Social proof placement | Pricing restructure |
| Form field removal | Multi-step form build |

| Low Impact + Low Effort | Low Impact + High Effort |
|------------------------|-------------------------|
| Button color | Animation/interaction |
| Footer changes | New section addition |
| Minor copy edits | New feature comparison table |
