# Paywall Upgrade CRO Experiment Ideas

Experiment catalog for paywall and upgrade flow optimization. Prioritize by stage: impression → click → checkout → retention.

---

## Paywall Presentation

**Copy and messaging**
- Value-led copy ("Unlock unlimited reports") vs. feature-led ("Get Pro plan")
- Urgency/scarcity: countdown timer vs. no countdown
- Social proof on paywall: "X users upgraded this week" vs. no social proof
- Testimonial from paying customer on paywall vs. no testimonial
- Pain-point headline ("Hitting your limit?") vs. benefit headline ("Do more with Pro")

**Visual treatment**
- Blur/dim locked content behind paywall vs. hard gate (content not visible)
- Show preview of locked content vs. show product mockup
- Animated paywall entrance vs. static
- Full-screen paywall modal vs. inline paywall section
- Dark overlay vs. light overlay

**Plan display**
- Show plans on paywall vs. redirect to pricing page
- Highlight recommended plan vs. no highlight
- Show savings calculation ("Save $120/year") vs. show price only
- Annual-first display vs. monthly-first display

---

## CTA and Conversion

**Primary CTA**
- "Upgrade now" vs. "Start Pro" vs. "Unlock [feature name]"
- CTA with trial offer ("Try Pro free for 7 days") vs. direct purchase CTA
- Single CTA vs. CTA + "Maybe later" secondary option
- Button color: high contrast accent vs. brand primary

**Risk reduction**
- "No credit card required" for trial vs. credit card required
- Money-back guarantee badge: near CTA vs. not shown
- Cancel anytime copy: near CTA vs. in plan description
- Free tier comparison: "See what you'd lose" vs. forward-looking "See what you'd gain"

---

## Pricing and Plan Structure

**Plan options**
- 2 tiers vs. 3 tiers on upgrade modal
- Include Free comparison column vs. only paid tiers
- Feature checklist per plan vs. "everything in Free, plus..." format
- Per-seat pricing display vs. flat pricing

**Trial offers**
- 7-day free trial vs. 14-day free trial
- Free trial vs. freemium upgrade nudge
- Credit card required for trial vs. no credit card
- Trial with full feature access vs. trial with limited features

---

## Frequency and Triggering

**Paywall trigger timing**
- Trigger on third usage of locked feature vs. first usage
- Trigger after X days of free use vs. usage-based trigger
- Show paywall on page load if usage limit hit vs. wait for user interaction
- Progressive nudges before hard paywall: 3-nudge sequence vs. 1-nudge vs. immediate hard paywall

**Soft gates (upgrade nudges)**
- Tooltip nudge vs. banner nudge vs. sidebar upgrade card
- Feature preview + lock icon vs. greyed-out feature
- Usage meter visible in nav vs. hidden until limit approached

---

## Post-Click (Checkout)

**Checkout flow**
- In-app checkout (no page navigation) vs. redirect to billing page
- Pre-fill email/name in checkout vs. empty form
- Plan confirmation screen before payment vs. go straight to payment
- Add billing FAQ inline vs. link to help doc

**Payment options**
- Credit card only vs. credit card + PayPal vs. + Apple/Google Pay
- Annual default-selected vs. monthly default-selected
- Show pro-rated cost for mid-cycle upgrade vs. hide

---

## Retention and Downgrade Prevention

**Cancel flow**
- Offer pause option before cancel vs. no pause option
- Offer discount at cancel attempt: 20% off vs. no discount
- Show "what you'll lose" list vs. just confirm cancellation
- Require cancellation reason vs. one-click cancel

**Reactivation**
- Email sequence timing: 1 day / 7 day / 30 day after downgrade
- Win-back offer: discount vs. new feature announcement
- Personalized win-back (feature they used most) vs. generic offer
